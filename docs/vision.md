# Vision

Nook is the go-to study platform — whether you're a student or a hobbyist —
designed to keep you **focused** and **engaged** in your studies and activities.

## Principles

- **Focus first.** The core experience is a calm, distraction-free study session.
- **Better together.** Study rooms make solo work feel shared and accountable.
- **Progress you can see.** Streaks and stats turn effort into motivation.

## Who it's for

Students, self-learners, and hobbyists who want structure and momentum without
the friction of heavyweight productivity tools.
